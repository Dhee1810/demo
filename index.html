About Our University

Bayalasi Snatkottar Mahavidyalaya came into existence in 1970 at Lucknow, the Capital of Uttar Pradesh, India. Today Bayalasi Snatkottar Mahavidyalaya is focused on following main educational areas: Medicine, Management, Teachers Training Institutions, Humanities, Computer Training Centres & Life Sciences and Madrasa Education. The institutions offer both foundation courses in these areas, as well as specialization with a Bachelors/ Masters Degree in select few.
